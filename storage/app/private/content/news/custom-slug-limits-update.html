Update: Monthly Limits for Custom Slugs

To keep PureLnk stable and prevent system abuse, we are introducing a monthly quota for custom aliases.

Starting today, the Free Plan includes up to 5 custom slugs per month. This limit only applies to links where you manually set a "custom code". Standard randomly generated links remain unlimited within your overall plan capacity.

You can track your custom slug usage in your dashboard. If you need more flexibility for your brand, stay tuned for our upcoming premium tiers!
